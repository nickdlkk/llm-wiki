# CodePilot

**A multi-model AI agent desktop client** — connect any AI provider, extend with MCP & skills, control from your phone, and let your assistant learn your workflow.

## Features

- **Multi-Provider Support**: Connect to 17+ AI providers out of the box, switch mid-conversation without losing context
- **MCP & Skills**: Extend functionality with Model Context Protocol and custom skills
- **Mobile Control**: Control your AI assistant from your phone
- **Cross-Platform**: macOS, Windows, Linux (arm64 + x64)

## Supported Providers

| Category | Providers |
|----------|-----------|
| Anthropic | Claude family |
