# plan-ceo-review

CEO/founder-mode plan review. Rethink the problem, find the 10-star product, challenge premises, expand scope when it creates a better product.

## Four Modes

1. **SCOPE EXPANSION** — Dream big
2. **SELECTIVE EXPANSION** — Hold scope + cherry-pick expansions
3. **HOLD SCOPE** — Maximum rigor
4. **SCOPE REDUCTION** — Strip to essentials

## When to Use

Use when asked to "think bigger", "expand scope", "strategy review", "rethink this", or "is this ambitious enough".

Proactively suggest when the user is questioning scope or ambition of a plan, or when the plan feels like it could be thinking bigger.
